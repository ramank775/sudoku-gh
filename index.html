How to Play Sudoku

The goal of Sudoku is to fill a 9√ó9 grid with digits so each column, row, and 3√ó3 section has numbers 1-9. Initially, some squares are filled.
Use logic to complete the grid, avoiding:

  • Duplicate numbers in any row
  • Duplicate numbers in any column
  • Duplicate numbers in any 3√ó3 grid

Mastering this puzzle can seem daunting for beginners, but fear not‚Äîwe've got tips to boost your skills.
