Information Collection and Use

The app is designed with privacy as a core principle. Most of your personal data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Local Data Storage

The following information is stored locally on your device and is not collected by us:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • User preferences and settings
  • Completed puzzles and achievements
  • Daily challenge streaks and history

Analytics Data Collection (New in v1.11.0)

Starting with version 1.11.0, we collect limited, privacy-focused analytics through our self-hosted Plausible Analytics server (analytics.one9x.com) to improve the app experience:

Data We Collect:

  • App Usage Metrics: App launches and session duration
  • Feature Usage: Which screens you visit (home, play, stats, etc.)
  • Game Mode Preferences: Usage of different game modes (classic, daily, timed, rush hour)
  • Game Completion Events: When games are completed and daily challenge streaks

Data We Do NOT Collect:

  • Individual cell inputs or game moves
  • Detailed gameplay patterns (undo/redo actions)
  • Personal identifiers or account information
  • Device-specific identifiers beyond what's necessary for analytics
  • Location data

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory.

This service does not use these "cookies" explicitly. However, the app may use third-party code and libraries that use "cookies" to collect information and improve their services. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse our cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of this service.
